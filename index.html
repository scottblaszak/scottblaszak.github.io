About

Scott Blaszak is a screenwriter whose recent thriller FIVE MEN DOWN was in development with 21 Laps. The script went wide in 2025, generating meetings with over a dozen major production companies. His sci-fi thriller PARALLEL was released in 2016.

Blaszak specializes in elevated thrillers that explore systemic corruption, moral compromise, and the human cost of ambition. His work examines American institutions—professional sports, corporate power, technology—through the lens of characters making impossible choices. Whether a desperate GM orchestrating tragedy to save his franchise or a fixer uncovering league-wide conspiracy, his protagonists navigate the gap between ideals and survival. His scripts combine the propulsive plotting of commercial thrillers with the character complexity and thematic depth of prestige drama.

He's currently working on finishing up his first book, DISRUPTIFICATION.
