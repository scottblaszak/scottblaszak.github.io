About the Book

LINUS DIBLEY was there for all of it. The first dot-com boom. The bust. The resurrection. Social media's rise. The smartphone revolution. Crypto. AI. Every tech disruption of the last forty years, and Linus J. Dibley had his fingerprints on it. Sometimes literally. Often disastrously. Now, in 2036, as his greatest creation threatens to destroy humanity, Linus has one last chance to set things right—and one hard drive containing the complete history of how the hell we got here.

Part satire, part cautionary tale, part love letter to the chaos of innovation, DISRUPTIFICATION is a rocket-paced romp through the tech industry's greatest hits and most catastrophic misses, as seen through the eyes of its most brilliant, most reckless, and most accidentally influential figure.

Think Forrest Gump meets The Social Network by way of Walk Hard.
